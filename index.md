I'm a software engineer, writer, athlete, and hobby-maximalist.  

In the summer of 2024, I completed a 3-month batch at the [Recurse Center](https://www.recurse.com/), a self-directed coding retreat. While I was there, I focused on developing research-level AI expertise. I spent my first 8 weeks working learning AI Safety and research fundamentals through [Alignment Research Engineer Accelerator](https://www.arena.education/) (ARENA) I spent the last month putting those skills to use in an independent research project investigating the use of [Sparse AutoEncoders (SAEs) to selectively remove information from large language models](https://www.kelechin.com/unlearning1).

After Recurse, I was selected to be a participant in Neel Nanda's [MATS](https://www.matsprogram.org/) training phase, where I worked on interpreting mechanisms of refusal in LLMs. Outside of AI research, I've worked on a desktop app to visualize personal time data and fine-tuning an LLM to sound like me.
